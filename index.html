01 About

A calm, careful hand for the documents that matter.

JChan Notary Services is an independent mobile notary practice serving Houston, Austin, Dallas, San Antonio, and their surrounding communities. The idea is simple: signing important documents shouldn't mean rearranging your whole day. So I bring the notarial seal to you.

Whether it's a loan closing at your kitchen table, an advance directive at a loved one's bedside, or an affidavit you need handled before the workday starts, I show up on time, prepared, and discreet. Every signature is properly identified, journaled, and sealed in line with Texas law.

You'll always know the cost before we begin, you'll receive an itemized receipt, and you'll never feel rushed.

7-dayEvening & weekend appointments by request

A notary public verifies identity and witnesses signatures. I am not an attorney and cannot prepare legal documents, select forms, or give legal advice. For those needs, please consult a licensed Texas attorney.

02 Services

What I can notarize for you.

Bring the unsigned document and a valid photo ID — I'll handle the rest. These are the documents clients ask for most.

General & Mobile Notarizations

Acknowledgments, jurats, oaths, and copy certifications — at the place and time that work for you.

Loan Signing & Real Estate

Refinances, purchases, and reverse-mortgage packages — careful, complete signings that keep your closing on track.

Wills & Testamentary Documents

Notarization and witnessing of self-proving affidavits and related estate-planning signatures, per the Texas Estates Code.

Power of Attorney Documents

Notarizing legal and financial power-of-attorney forms so your representative can act when it counts.

Affidavits & Sworn Statements

Administering the oath and notarizing sworn affidavits, declarations, and statements of fact.

Healthcare Directives & Living Wills

Notarizing medical powers of attorney and advance directives — bedside and care-facility visits welcome.

Copy Certification

Certified true copies of original documents you hold as custodian, when an institution requires one.

I-9 Employment Verification

Acting as an authorized representative to review documents and complete Section 2 for remote new hires.
